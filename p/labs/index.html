Lab assignments

The following lab assignments will not be changed. You can attend each lab and demo to your TA before the end of your lab session. No late lab assignment will be accepted. You must attend your assigned lab to earn your credits. You don't need to hand in source codes or makefile to TA after you are done with the demo in each lab.

You can use SSH and XWin32 so that you can work at home/hall. Details are here.

We ask you to put effort in the labs. However, if you cannot finish the lab within the lab session, you may explain your code and problems to your TA, and s/he will give you partial or full scores depending on situation. We encourage you to complete any unfinished lab assignments after lab session because all projects are closely related to the labs.

Alternatively, you can choose the batch mode to score your lab assignments. Nonetheless, you remain to enrol in COMP2012H LA1A in compliance of the University's course registration policy.

The supported development environment is g++ under Linux. You're welcome to do the lab assignments elsewhere, but make sure your program compiles and runs on the machines in Lab 2 because the TA will grade your labs there.
